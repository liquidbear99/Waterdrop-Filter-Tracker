# Waterdrop Filter Tracker

![Waterdrop Filter Tracker icon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/liquidbear99/Waterdrop-Filter-Tracker/main/custom_components/waterdrop_filter_tracker/brand/icon.png)

Track Waterdrop filter replacement timing inside Home Assistant.

This integration creates sensors for days remaining, life remaining, estimated
gallons used, gallons remaining, and estimated replacement date.

Preset reverse osmosis filters are available for Waterdrop X12 F1A, F2, and
X12-F3 RO membrane replacement schedules, while Custom remains available for
other reverse osmosis filters.
